Mercedes-AMG C63 S  W205 · 2015–2021
Community Verified

Known Issues by Mileage

Rear diff bushing wear — 80,000km+
Clunking on hard acceleration is common. Check for play in the subframe. Replacement is $1,200–$1,800 at an independent specialist.
Cam phaser rattle on cold start — 60,000km+
The M177 can rattle briefly on cold starts. Listen carefully for the first 5 seconds. Persistent rattle needs attention before it escalates.
DSG transmission software — all years
Ensure the latest TCU software is flashed. Rough shifts at low speed are often a software issue, not mechanical.
Panoramic sunroof drain blockage
Common on all W205. Water ingress into the boot or cabin. Easy fix — clear the drains at each corner. Check for staining on the headliner.
